## Real Pilot Blockers

Do not convert this demo into a live process until:

- Authorising body is confirmed.
- Governance charter is approved.
- Data sovereignty charter is approved.
- Cultural safety boundaries are approved.
- Eligibility rules are approved.
- Moderators/admins are ready.
- Backup/restore is tested.
- Privacy notice is reviewed.
