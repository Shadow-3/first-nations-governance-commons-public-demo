# Controlled Sample Runbook: Barayamal and First Nations Toastmasters v5.2

## Barayamal

Use fake founder, business owner, mentor, program, resource, and feedback records only. Block real names, ABNs, revenue, private business issues, contact details, cultural material, and business-sensitive records.

## First Nations Toastmasters

Use fake guest, prospective-member, and training-interest records only. Block member numbers, payments, education records, officer roles, elections, incidents, official club records, and official Toastmasters systems.

## Acceptance

Samples can be marked accepted only after private owners confirm no real intake, official workflow, or sensitive data was touched.
