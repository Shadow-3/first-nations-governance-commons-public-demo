## Routing Rule

Every advisory note must be routed to an accountable owner. Dean comments can improve the build, test cases, and wording, but cannot close authority, privacy, IDS/data, support, official Toastmasters, or live launch gates.

## Public Boundary

Public outputs can say that advisory review was requested or completed. They must not publish private evidence, real participant records, owner decisions, support details, or approval claims.
