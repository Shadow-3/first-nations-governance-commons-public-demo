## Automatic No-Go Risks For Real Pilot

Do not proceed to a real pilot if any are unresolved:

- Authorising body unclear.
- Eligibility/voting rules unclear.
- Cultural safety boundaries not approved.
- Data ownership/hosting/export/deletion unclear.
- Privacy notice missing.
- Local admins not identified.
- Offline participation process untested.
- Partner boundaries unclear.

## Recommended Risk Review Rhythm

- Review before advisor outreach.
- Review after advisor feedback.
- Review before Decidim sandbox.
- Review before any real pilot.
