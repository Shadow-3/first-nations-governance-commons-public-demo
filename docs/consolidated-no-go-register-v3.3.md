# Consolidated No-go Register v3.3

This register combines blockers from the public demo, organisation onboarding, private pilot readiness, operations, implementation, launch, closeout, authority approval, and activation drill layers.

## Blocked triggers

- NG-001: Public data leak - Any real name, email, phone, address, access need, incident detail, payment, member number, signature, authority record, or official Toastmasters record in public files
- NG-002: Authority missing - No named First Nations authority or quorum for activation decision
- NG-003: IDS review missing - Data classes, purpose, access, retention, deletion, or reporting not approved
- NG-004: Privacy signoff missing - Notice, consent, withdrawal, correction, PIA, retention, or incident response is incomplete
- NG-005: Private environment unapproved - No approved workspace, access register, MFA, backup, export, delete, restore, or logs
- NG-006: Access too broad - No-private-access roles, provider, supporter, or public reviewer can see private records
- NG-007: Official Toastmasters boundary broken - Forms, member numbers, signatures, payments, or charter records enter public/pilot package
- NG-008: Incident pause path missing - No owner can pause intake, support participant, or run breach/cultural safety escalation
- NG-009: Withdrawal/correction fails - Participant cannot withdraw or correct private record
- NG-010: Provider receives real data too early - Provider gets real export before authority, contract, privacy, and access pass
- NG-015: Scale starts without closeout - Second pilot, provider handoff, or broader rollout starts before closeout and authority record
- NG-016: Decision record incomplete - Go/hold/no-go record lacks conditions, expiry, owner, no-go triggers, or monitoring schedule
- NG-017: First-72-hour monitor absent - No hour 0-72 checks or authority checkpoint after activation

## Hold triggers

- NG-011: Public learning identifies someone - Small sample or contextual wording can identify a founder, participant, support need, or incident
- NG-012: Backup/delete/restore untested - Team cannot prove recovery, export, deletion, or retention pathway
- NG-013: Support route unstaffed - No response owner or response clock for support, withdrawal, correction, or access needs
- NG-014: Closeout trigger missing - Pilot has no review date, retention/deletion rule, or closeout owner
- NG-018: Control tower not reviewed - No single review of stage status, dependencies, blockers, owners, and evidence before decision

## Rule

No blocked trigger can be waived in public. It needs private authority, data, privacy, technical, safety, or official-route evidence before any real activation decision.
