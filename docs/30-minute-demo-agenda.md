# 30-Minute Demo Agenda

Status: v0.1 facilitation guide  
Project: First Nations Governance Commons  
Demo mode: public demo with fake/sample data only

## Purpose

Use this agenda to run a focused advisor, funder, or builder walkthrough of the public demo.

## Before The Demo

Send:

- Public demo link.
- Advisor Review Guide.
- Feedback Form.
- Go/No-Go Scorecard.

Confirm:

- This is fake/sample data only.
- It is not a real community consultation.
- The goal is review, not endorsement.

## Agenda

| Time | Topic | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 0-3 min | Context | Barayamal as initial developer/custodian; communities control authority/data/rules. |
| 3-6 min | Problem | Consultation fatigue, weak accountability, unclear mandate, offline exclusion. |
| 6-10 min | Demo overview | Show public demo, process phases, fake-data label. |
| 10-14 min | Participation flow | Proposals, survey idea, meetings, yarning circle import, support/vote concept. |
| 14-18 min | Mandate Record | Explain why the core output is evidence of authority and accountability. |
| 18-21 min | Readiness gates | Show public demo passed, sandbox next, advisor review required, real pilot blocked. |
| 21-24 min | Accountability and evidence | Show tracker, evidence pack, partner boundary. |
| 24-28 min | Advisor questions | Legitimacy, safety, data, offline access, capture risk, usefulness. |
| 28-30 min | Next step | Ask for scorecard and top 5 changes. |

## Demo Script

Opening:

"This is a fake-data public demo. It is not a live Decidim instance and it is not a real community consultation. The purpose is to test whether the model is legitimate, safe, useful, and worth configuring as a Decidim sandbox."

Core framing:

"Barayamal can build and maintain the infrastructure, but communities must control their own authority, decision rules, data, and cultural boundaries."

Mandate Record framing:

"The core product is not the website. The core product is the Mandate Record: what was asked, who authorised it, who participated, what was decided, who must act, and what changed."

Closing ask:

"I am not asking for endorsement. I am asking what must change before this should become a Decidim sandbox or a real pilot."

## Questions To Ask Live

- What feels strong?
- What feels unsafe or unclear?
- What would stop this being trusted?
- What must never be public?
- What would make the evidence useful?
- Should the next step be sandbox, revision, or pause?
