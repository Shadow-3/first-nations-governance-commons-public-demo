# Advisor Review Guide

Status: v0.1 review pack  
Project: First Nations Governance Commons  
Initial developer/custodian: Barayamal / Dean Foley  
Demo mode: public demo with fake/sample data only

## Purpose

Use this guide to review whether the public demo is ready to become a Decidim sandbox, and what must change before any real community pilot.

The review should focus on legitimacy, cultural safety, data sovereignty, offline participation, and accountability.

## Recommended Review Group

Invite 3-5 reviewers across:

- First Nations governance experience.
- Community engagement or facilitation.
- Data sovereignty/privacy.
- Youth or digital inclusion.
- Technical/product delivery.
- Cultural safety or local protocol knowledge where appropriate.

## Demo Walkthrough

1. Open `index.html`.
2. Confirm the fake-data warning is visible.
3. Review the process phases.
4. Filter proposals by category.
5. Test the support button.
6. Review meetings and offline input.
7. Review the Mandate Record.
8. Review launch gates and open decisions.
9. Review the accountability tracker.
10. Open the evidence pack links.

## Review Questions

### 1. Legitimacy

- Would the process be trusted as a real community decision pathway?
- Who would need to authorise a real process?
- What decision rights are missing?
- What would make the Mandate Record credible?

### 2. Cultural Safety

- What should never be uploaded?
- Which spaces should be public, member-only, restricted, or offline-only?
- Who reviews cultural concerns?
- What language should be changed before showing community members?

### 3. Data Sovereignty

- Who owns the data?
- Where should the data be hosted?
- What data should be avoided entirely?
- What retention, deletion, and export rules are needed?
- What should be included in the privacy notice?

### 4. Offline Participation

- Are yarning circles, paper forms, phone input, and facilitator notes treated as equal participation?
- What should the offline upload template capture?
- How should facilitator summaries be checked?
- What should remain offline?

### 5. Accountability

- Does the tracker show who must act and by when?
- What partner response rules are needed?
- What statuses are missing?
- Would the evidence pack help in advocacy, funding, or board/governance meetings?

### 6. Capture Risks

- How could funders, governments, companies, consultants, or vocal online users distort the process?
- What safeguards should be stronger?
- What access should partners have?
- What should partners never control?

## Go/No-Go Recommendation

Choose one:

- **Go to Decidim sandbox**: fake-data sandbox can be configured.
- **Revise public demo first**: demo needs changes before sandbox work.
- **Pause for governance review**: authority/data/cultural issues need more work.
- **Not suitable**: model does not fit the intended use.

## Required Review Output

After the review, produce:

- Top 5 changes needed.
- Go/no-go decision for Decidim sandbox.
- Open risks.
- Suggested first pilot scope.
- People who should review the next version.
