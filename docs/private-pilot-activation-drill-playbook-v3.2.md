# Private Pilot Activation Drill Playbook v3.2

Mode: public fake/sample activation drill only.

## Review finding

The v3.1 authority package defined what must be approved, but it still needed an activation rehearsal that proves the decision can be run, paused, documented, and monitored before real intake opens.

## Activation rule

Do not open real private pilot intake until the activation drill, signoff matrix, tabletop scenarios, evidence packet, rehearsal tasks, first-72-hour monitoring checks, and recorded go/hold/no-go decision have passed.

## Drill sequence

1. Confirm authority quorum and developer custody boundary.
2. Read the approved pilot scope and official Toastmasters separation aloud.
3. Review IDS/data governance pass and hold items.
4. Review privacy impact, notice, consent, withdrawal, retention, and deletion.
5. Test role access, no-private-access roles, backup/export/delete, and restore.
6. Run incident, breach, cultural safety, support, and official handoff tabletop scenarios.
7. Run public package redaction QA.
8. Record go, hold, or no-go with conditions, expiry, review date, and stop triggers.
9. Schedule first-72-hour monitoring before activation.

## Evidence rule

Public files can show only fake/sample status, aggregate counts, and public-safe summaries. Authority records, access logs, incident evidence, support details, withdrawal/correction evidence, private decision records, member numbers, payment details, signatures, and official Toastmasters records stay in the approved private environment.

## No-go triggers

- Real intake opens before the activation drill, tabletop scenarios, and signoff matrix are completed.
- Authority decision is recorded without quorum, delegation, conditions, expiry, or stop triggers.
- Private pilot records, access logs, incident evidence, official Toastmasters records, payment details, member numbers, or support details appear in public files.
- No-private-access roles, providers, public reviewers, or supporters can view private records.
- Withdrawal, correction, support, incident pause, breach assessment, backup/restore, deletion, or public redaction QA fails.
- Official Toastmasters forms, signatures, payments, member numbers, or charter records enter the public package or private pilot tool.
- Activation continues past the first 72 hours without authority checkpoint, monitoring evidence, or closeout trigger.
