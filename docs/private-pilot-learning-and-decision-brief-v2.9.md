## Decision Evidence

The closeout decision should include which launch gates passed, held, or failed; whether any no-go trigger occurred; founder and demo movement at aggregate level; whether official Toastmasters handoff stayed outside the public package; whether withdrawals, access support, incidents, and corrections met response clocks; whether public evidence stayed fake/sample; and what records were deleted, retained, or handed off.

## Public Summary Template

Use only aggregate, redacted language: pilot status, public-safe learning, aggregate counts where safe, and next steps. Do not include identities, contact details, meeting links, access needs, incidents, official documents, payment details, member numbers, or authority records.

## Decision Rule

Proceed only if the launch gates, public evidence boundary, official handoff boundary, access support, withdrawal, incident, deletion, and closeout evidence are satisfactory. Hold or no-go if any no-go trigger occurred or if the pilot cannot prove what was collected, who accessed it, what was deleted, and what was handed off.
