# Private Pilot Launch Playbook v2.9

Release: v2.9 Private Pilot Launch Control  
Initial developer/custodian: Barayamal / Dean Foley  
Mode: public fake/sample launch-control template only

## Purpose

This playbook turns the v2.8 private pilot implementation package into a controlled launch-and-learning workflow for the Toastmasters First Nations controlled sample.

The public package can show the launch method. It must not collect or expose real private pilot records.

## Launch Rule

Do not open controlled real intake until v2.8 implementation gates pass, the private environment is the only system of record, v2.9 launch gates are approved, and privacy notice, consent, withdrawal, access, incident, official handoff, retention, deletion, and closeout rules are live.

## 30-Day Launch Sequence

### Week 0: Go/No-Go

- Confirm authority to open controlled intake.
- Smoke-test the private environment with fake records.
- Verify privacy notice, withdrawal route, named access, communications, and public wording.
- Record proceed, hold, or no-go.

### Week 1: Open Quietly

- Open controlled expression of interest through the private route only.
- Send EOI receipts and demo invites privately.
- Route contacts into founder, demo-first, supporter, access support, withdrawal, or official handoff states.
- Run the first weekly learning review.

### Week 2: Founder Readiness

- Check founder candidates without counting supporters or demo-first attendees as charter-ready founders.
- Keep official Toastmasters forms, signatures, member numbers, payments, and acceptance records outside the public package.
- Review incident, access support, withdrawal, and operator load metrics.

### Week 3: Handoff And Evidence

- Prepare official handoff outside Governance Commons public files.
- Produce public-safe aggregate progress only after redaction QA.
- Close high-risk defects before any public update.

### Week 4: Closeout Decision

- Record proceed, hold, or no-go.
- Confirm deletion, correction, retention, and handoff evidence.
- Publish only aggregate learning and public-safe next steps.

## No-Go Triggers

- Any real private field appears in public pages, CSVs, screenshots, JSON, GitHub issues, or the fake-data Decidim sandbox.
- Controlled intake opens before the privacy notice, consent route, withdrawal route, access register, private environment smoke test, and go/no-go decision are approved.
- Meeting links, access needs, incidents, official Toastmasters records, payment details, member numbers, or authority records are exposed publicly.
- Supporters, mentors, sponsors, demo-first attendees, or unsure contacts are counted as charter-ready founders without confirmation.
- Weekly learning review or closeout decision is skipped.

## Evidence To Keep Private

- Intake records and contact details.
- Meeting links and attendance lists.
- Consent, withdrawal, correction, and deletion records.
- Official Toastmasters paperwork and member identifiers.
- Access support and incident details.
- Access register and private environment configuration.

## Evidence Safe For Public Demo

- Fake/sample launch gate states.
- Aggregate counts where sample size and privacy are safe.
- Redacted proceed, hold, or no-go decision summary.
- Public-safe lessons, next steps, and template improvements.
