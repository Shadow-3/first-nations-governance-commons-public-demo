## How To Use

Score each provider from 0-5 for each category.

- 0 = not addressed
- 1 = weak
- 2 = partial
- 3 = acceptable
- 4 = strong
- 5 = excellent

## Automatic No-Go Conditions

Do not proceed with a provider if:

- They propose using real community data in the sandbox.
- They cannot explain hosting, backups, updates, and admin handover.
- They treat governance, privacy, data sovereignty, or cultural safety as optional.
- They cannot provide clear scope, cost, and timeline assumptions.
- They require a proprietary lock-in path without explaining risks and exit options.

## Scoring Guide

Maximum weighted score: 500.

- 400-500: strong candidate.
- 300-399: possible candidate; clarify weak areas.
- 200-299: high risk; proceed only with major revisions.
- Under 200: do not proceed.
