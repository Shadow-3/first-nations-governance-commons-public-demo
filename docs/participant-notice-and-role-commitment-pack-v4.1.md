# Participant Notice And Role Commitment Pack v4.1

## Safety rule

This v4.1 package is public fake/sample data only. Do not collect or publish real names, emails, phone numbers, founder or business details, member numbers, payments, signatures, authority minutes, consent evidence, access logs, support records, incident details, cultural material, official Toastmasters records, or private pilot evidence.

## Notice clauses

- NOT-001: Public safe / Ready - What this pilot is - This is a controlled private pilot only after approvals pass.
- NOT-002: Public safe / Ready - What this pilot is not - It is not an official Toastmasters record system or public consultation.
- NOT-003: Private restricted / Blocked - Who can approve participation - Only approved private authority owners can approve real participation.
- NOT-004: Private restricted / Blocked - Data collected - Only approved minimum data is collected privately.
- NOT-005: Public safe / Ready - Data not collected - Public demo does not collect real names, emails, member numbers, payments, or signatures.
- NOT-006: Private restricted / Blocked - Consent and withdrawal - Consent, withdrawal, and support route are handled privately.
- NOT-007: Private restricted / Hold - Support and pause - Support and pause routes are available before real intake.
- NOT-008: Public safe / Hold - Official records - Official Toastmasters records remain external.
- NOT-009: Private restricted / Hold - Access and roles - Access is role-based and revocable.
- NOT-010: Private restricted / Blocked - Retention and deletion - Retention and deletion follow private data governance approval.
- NOT-011: Public safe / Ready - Public updates - Public updates are aggregate and fake/sample safe.
- NOT-012: Private restricted / Hold - Complaints and incidents - Complaints, incidents, and affected-person details stay private.

## Role commitments

- ROL-001: Organisation lead - Hold - can: Coordinate fake-data rehearsal and private pilot readiness. - cannot: Approve First Nations authority alone.
- ROL-002: Founder participant - Hold - can: Use private approved forms and meetings. - cannot: Enter sensitive data in public demo.
- ROL-003: Business owner participant - Hold - can: Submit private pilot feedback if approved. - cannot: Share confidential commercial records publicly.
- ROL-004: Mentor - Hold - can: View approved material and give advice. - cannot: Access private evidence by default.
- ROL-005: Partner observer - Ready - can: View public updates. - cannot: Export private records.
- ROL-006: Dean trusted reviewer - Ready - can: Review evidence boundaries and developer custody. - cannot: Approve authority/privacy/IDS/official route.
- ROL-007: Member tester - Blocked - can: Use sample meeting and learning flows. - cannot: Store member number or official records.
- ROL-008: Guest tester - Hold - can: View public meeting/welcome material. - cannot: Be treated as official member.
- ROL-009: Club facilitator - Hold - can: Moderate sample pages and meetings. - cannot: Make official Toastmasters decisions in Decidim.
- ROL-010: Authority chair - Blocked - can: Approve, hold, archive, or request repeat rehearsal. - cannot: Publish private minutes.
- ROL-011: Privacy reviewer - Blocked - can: Hold launch if privacy risks remain. - cannot: Publish privacy evidence with identities.
- ROL-012: IDS/data governance reviewer - Blocked - can: Approve private data handling. - cannot: Put data governance evidence in public repo.
- ROL-013: Technical custodian - Hold - can: Operate private sandbox under controls. - cannot: Share credentials or logs publicly.
- ROL-014: Comms lead - Ready - can: Share public learning update. - cannot: Overstate readiness or publish private evidence.
