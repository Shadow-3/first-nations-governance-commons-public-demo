# Barayamal Founder And Business Owner Onboarding Test Plan v3.7

Barayamal is the primary organisation test track.

## Audience

Indigenous founders, business owners, mentors, program participants, and approved partners.

## Fake-data stages

- ONB-002: Founder and business owner interest path - Configure - Collect fake interest categories and support needs.
- ONB-003: Indigenous business owner profile rehearsal - Configure - Test business stage, sector, support priority, and referral category using sample records.
- ONB-004: Mentor and advisor pathway - Configure - Test fake mentor role matching and meeting availability.
- ONB-005: Program proposal and support request - Configure - Let sample founders propose support topics and vote-like priorities.
- ONB-006: Partner and funder observer route - Hold - Allow approved observers to read public-safe progress only.
- ONB-007: Founder accountability follow-up - Configure - Track fake follow-up actions by owner type and due date.

## Barayamal-specific segments

- SEG-001: Indigenous founder - Private restricted - do not collect: Real name, email, phone, business finances, hardship details.
- SEG-002: Indigenous business owner - Private restricted - do not collect: ABN, revenue, customer data, contracts, tax, legal matters.
- SEG-003: Program participant - Private restricted - do not collect: Eligibility documents, identity evidence, contact details.
- SEG-004: Mentor or advisor - Restricted - do not collect: Credentials, conflicts, background checks, contact details.
- SEG-005: Partner or funder observer - Hold - do not collect: Private records, exports, participant identities.
- SEG-006: Barayamal admin - Restricted - do not collect: Authority, privacy, IDS, or official Toastmasters approval power.

## Barayamal acceptance tests

- TST-002: Submit fake founder interest - Required - Sample founder/business owner interest record can be created and exported as fake data.
- TST-003: Answer support topic proposal - Required - Admin answers a fake proposal and links an accountability action.
- TST-004: Publish founder yarning summary - Required - Meeting minutes publish without attendee or private support details.
- TST-005: Partner observer cannot access private records - Blocked - Observer role sees public updates only.
- TST-006: Mentor role held until approval - Hold - Mentor matching remains disabled until conflict/privacy rules pass.
