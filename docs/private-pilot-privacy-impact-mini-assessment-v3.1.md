# Private Pilot Privacy Impact Mini Assessment v3.1

Release: v3.1 Private Pilot Authority Approval  
Source basis: OAIC Australian Privacy Principles and OAIC privacy impact assessment guidance.

## Assessment Questions

- What information is collected and why?
- Which fields are not collected?
- Who can access each data class?
- Where is the private workspace hosted?
- How can a participant withdraw or correct information?
- What is deleted, retained, archived, or transferred after closeout?
- What happens if data is exposed, inaccurate, or used beyond purpose?
- What public reporting threshold prevents identity inference?

## Minimum Decision

Real pilot activation should stay on hold until required privacy checks pass or have named hold actions, owners, and review dates.
