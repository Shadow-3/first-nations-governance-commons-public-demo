# Private Authority Meeting Pack v3.4

Use this pack only in the approved private environment.

## Meeting sequence

- AM-001: Open fake/sample boundary (Required) - Confirm no public page or public file contains real private pilot evidence
- AM-002: Confirm authority quorum (Blocked) - Confirm who can approve and who cannot
- AM-003: Receive Dean advisory note (Ready) - Treat Dean note as advisory input, not approval
- AM-004: Review evidence vault completeness (Required) - Check every evidence item status and classification
- AM-005: IDS/data governance decision (Blocked) - Approve or hold data classes, purpose, access, retention, deletion, reporting
- AM-006: Privacy decision (Blocked) - Approve or hold notice, consent, withdrawal, correction, incident, retention
- AM-007: Role access decision (Blocked) - Approve or hold access register, MFA, roles, revoke test
- AM-008: Private environment decision (Blocked) - Approve or hold workspace, backups, export, delete, restore, logging
- AM-009: Official Toastmasters boundary decision (Hold) - Confirm official records and chartering route stay external
- AM-010: Incident and support decision (Hold) - Confirm pause, support, escalation, breach assessment, response clocks
- AM-011: Provider handoff decision (Hold) - Confirm no production export and provider fake-data lane
- AM-012: Monitoring decision (Hold) - Confirm first-72-hour checks and authority checkpoint
- AM-013: Conditions and expiry (Required) - Name conditions, owner, expiry, stop triggers, closeout trigger
- AM-014: Go/hold/no-go decision (Blocked) - Record limited private pilot, conditional hold, or no-go

## Decision rule

Do not open real intake, activate a private workspace, hand data to a provider, or scale the pilot until the evidence vault, advisory review boundary, authority meeting record, access rules, and decision gates are approved in a separate private environment.

## Required record fields

- Decision: go, hold, or no-go.
- Authority owner and quorum.
- Evidence gaps.
- Conditions.
- Expiry.
- Stop triggers.
- Monitoring schedule.
- Closeout/archive trigger.
