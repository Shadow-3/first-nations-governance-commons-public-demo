# Private Owner Agenda and Minute Template v5.5

The private meeting should record:

1. Chair, alternates, quorum, and scope.
2. One decision per P0 closeout item.
3. Evidence placeholder accepted, held, or rejected.
4. Stop trigger and failure action.
5. Public-safe line approved by communications.
6. Final owner decision: proceed to repeat run, repeat remediation, or no-go/archive.

Dean Foley / Barayamal is advisory and trusted reviewer only. Advisory review cannot replace required owner approval.
