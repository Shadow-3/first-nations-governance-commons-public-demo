# Control Tower Executive Review Agenda v3.3

## Purpose

Give advisors and decision owners one public-safe review route before any real First Nations Toastmasters private-pilot decision.

## Agenda

1. Confirm public fake/sample boundary.
2. Review stage readiness.
3. Review blocked no-go triggers.
4. Review authority, IDS, privacy, support, official Toastmasters, provider, and monitoring dependencies.
5. Assign owner actions.
6. Choose one decision route: public demo only, hold for authority, hold for activation drill, prepare private environment, provider quote with fake data, no-go real activation, conditional activation, or approve limited private pilot.
7. Record public-safe next status.

## Public wording rule

Do not publish private evidence, participant details, official Toastmasters records, access logs, incident facts, authority records, or real decision records.
