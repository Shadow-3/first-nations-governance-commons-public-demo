# Decidim Hardening and Evidence Traceability Plan v5.1

## Hardening Checks

- Outbound email is disabled or safely captured.
- Fake/sample records can be imported, exported, deleted, archived, restored, and wiped.
- Role assignment and revoke proof works.
- Provider access is least privilege and time limited.
- Logs, credentials, and private evidence do not enter the public package.
- Mobile, keyboard, and document viewer flows remain usable.

## Traceability

Each stress scenario links back to a v5.0 evidence gate, owner, private proof, public replacement line, and next review trigger.
