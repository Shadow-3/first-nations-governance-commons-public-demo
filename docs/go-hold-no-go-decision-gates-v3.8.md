# Go Hold No-Go Decision Gates v3.8

## Decision rule

A real private pilot remains blocked until every required fake-data script passes, critical and high issues are closed or accepted by the private authority process, evidence is redacted, admin revocation is proven, official Toastmasters records remain external, and private authority/privacy/IDS/data governance approvals are recorded outside this public package.

## Gates

## GAT-001 - Fake data only gate

State: Required

Evidence: All seeds, screenshots, CSV, JSON, docs use fake/sample data.

Go rule: No real record present.

Hold/no-go rule: Any real record blocks release.

## GAT-002 - Barayamal lane pass

State: Required

Evidence: Required Barayamal scripts pass or have approved hold.

Go rule: Founder/business owner path is testable.

Hold/no-go rule: Private or commercial details requested.

## GAT-003 - FN Toastmasters lane pass

State: Required

Evidence: Required member/guest scripts pass.

Go rule: Official boundary is visible and enforced.

Hold/no-go rule: Official records appear in Decidim.

## GAT-004 - Role and revocation gate

State: Required

Evidence: Fake admin, reviewer, observer, guest, and member roles tested.

Go rule: Revoked role loses access.

Hold/no-go rule: Any role keeps excess access.

## GAT-005 - Observer-note gate

State: Configure

Evidence: Observer prompts are public-safe and useful.

Go rule: Notes produce improvement signal.

Hold/no-go rule: Prompts collect private identity or support details.

## GAT-006 - Issue triage gate

State: Required

Evidence: Critical/high issues closed or formally held in private authority process.

Go rule: No unowned blocker remains.

Hold/no-go rule: Unowned critical/high issue remains.

## GAT-007 - Support and pause gate

State: Blocked

Evidence: Fake support pause tabletop completed in private route.

Go rule: Pause route found without public incident details.

Hold/no-go rule: Incident details appear in public.

## GAT-008 - Backup/export/delete gate

State: Hold

Evidence: Private proof exists for backup, export, delete, restore, and closeout.

Go rule: Public only shows pass/fail version.

Hold/no-go rule: No restore or deletion proof.

## GAT-009 - Private approval gate

State: Blocked

Evidence: First Nations authority, privacy, IDS/data governance, support, official-route approvals recorded privately.

Go rule: Approval references exist outside public package.

Hold/no-go rule: Public demo used as approval record.

## GAT-010 - Launch decision gate

State: Blocked

Evidence: Authority chooses go, hold, no-go, archive, or second fake-data iteration.

Go rule: Decision references evidence pack.

Hold/no-go rule: No authority decision.
