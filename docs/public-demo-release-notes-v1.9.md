Date: 2026-06-04

## Summary

v1.9 adds the Assurance Room, a sign-off and safety gate surface for deciding whether the fake-data Decidim sandbox is demo-ready, handover-ready, or blocked.

## Improved

- Public navigation now includes Assurance Room.
- Review Pack includes assurance-team pathways.
- Document viewer includes v1.9 assurance docs and data.
- Manifest, README, and package index now describe the v1.9 assurance layer.

## Why It Matters

v1.8 made the sprint runnable. v1.9 makes the outcome reviewable. It gives maintainers and advisors a clear place to decide:

- Can this fake-data sandbox be shown?
- Can it be handed over?
- What evidence is missing?
- What would force a no-go?
- Who still needs to sign off?

## Safety Boundary

This remains fake/sample data only. A real pilot still requires separate community authority, privacy, data sovereignty, cultural safety, eligibility, moderation, hosting, and support approvals.
