# Pilot Agreement And Organisation Onboarding Playbook v4.1

## Review finding

v4.0 can close the private fake-data rehearsal, but the project still needed a public-safe pack that helps First Nations organisations and controlled member testers join under approved terms if private gates pass.

## Operating rule

Use the Pilot Agreement & Organisation Onboarding Pack after the v4.0 closeout board. It converts a private fake-data closeout decision into public-safe joining terms, role commitments, participant notices, data schedules, support protocols, training steps, launch communications, and go/hold/no-go conditions.

## How to use this pack

1. Keep the current public decision at hold.
2. Complete the v4.0 private rehearsal closeout.
3. Use this pack as the template for private agreement drafting.
4. Keep all authority, consent, privacy, IDS/data governance, official Toastmasters, access, support, incident, payment, and signature evidence outside the public repository.
5. Publish only public-safe joining information after private approval.
