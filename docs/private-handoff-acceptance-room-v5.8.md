# Private Handoff Acceptance Room v5.8

v5.8 turns the v5.7 handoff packet into a private owner acceptance layer.

Use it after v5.7 to accept, hold, or block owner actions, custody items, repeat-run gates, public release lines, launch locks, decision options, and export packets.

The room cannot approve launch. It keeps limited private pilot, provider real-record access, outbound email, official Toastmasters workflows, and live Decidim blocked until all private gates pass.
