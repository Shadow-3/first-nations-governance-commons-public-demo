# First Nations Organisation Join Pathway v4.1

## Launch rule

First Nations organisations can join only through a private approved pathway after authority, privacy, IDS/data governance, official-route, support, technical, access, retention, and closeout approvals pass. The public pack provides templates and boundaries, not approval itself.

## Join pathways

- JON-001: Barayamal - Founder private pilot participant - Hold - required before: Authority, privacy, IDS, support, and field review. - boundary: No real names or business-sensitive details in public.
- JON-002: Barayamal - Business owner learning path - Hold - required before: Privacy/business field review. - boundary: No private commercial records.
- JON-003: Barayamal - Mentor or advisor path - Hold - required before: Conflict, privacy, and least-privilege rules. - boundary: No private participant records.
- JON-004: Barayamal - Partner observer path - Ready - required before: Observer-only access proof. - boundary: No private exports.
