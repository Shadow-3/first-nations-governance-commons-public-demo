# Incident and Breach Tabletop Script v3.2

Mode: fake/sample tabletop only.

## Scenario prompts

- A real email appears in a public CSV.
- A participant withdraws and asks what happened to their data.
- A provider asks for a production export.
- A Toastmasters member number is entered into the pilot form.
- A support request includes sensitive details.
- A Decidim admin role is granted to the wrong account.
- A public learning note risks identifying a founder.

## Tabletop questions

1. Who can pause intake immediately?
2. What evidence must stay private?
3. What public status can be shared safely?
4. Does the incident require legal/privacy advice or notification assessment?
5. What must be deleted, corrected, retained, or escalated?
6. What no-go trigger applies?

## Output

Record only public-safe status in this package. Keep incident facts, affected people, access logs, and decision records private.
