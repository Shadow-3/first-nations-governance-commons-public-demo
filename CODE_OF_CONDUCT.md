# Code Of Conduct

## Standard

Contributors must act with respect, care, and humility.

This project concerns First Nations governance infrastructure. Contributions must not extract, publish, pressure, or misrepresent community knowledge, authority, data, or cultural material.

## Expected Behaviour

- Use respectful language.
- Accept correction where cultural, data, privacy, or governance risks are raised.
- Keep discussion focused on the public demo, fake-data sandbox, and infrastructure.
- Make uncertainty visible.
- Protect people and communities from avoidable harm.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Uploading real community records, member data, cultural material, or sensitive information.
- Claiming the demo represents a real mandate or real decision.
- Harassment, abuse, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Pressuring people to disclose private or culturally restricted information.
- Bypassing fake-data, privacy, or cultural safety boundaries.

## Enforcement

The initial maintainer may close issues, remove content, block contributors, or pause contribution activity where safety, privacy, or governance boundaries are at risk.

This code of conduct supports the public repository only. It does not define real community authority or dispute resolution.
